Math class
==========
Math.abs(-100) ==> 100
Math.abs(100) ==> 100

Math.ceil(3.000001) ==> 4
Math.floor(3.9999) ==> 3

Math.tan(45)  == > 1.xxxxx

Math.pow(2,3) ==>  8
Math.max(1,2,3,4,5,6) ==>  6
Math.min(1,2,3,4,5,6) ==>  1

Math.sign(-100) ==> -1
Math.sign(100)  ==>  1
Math.sign(0)    ==> 0
